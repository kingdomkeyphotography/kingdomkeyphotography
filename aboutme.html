About Me

I am a Vietnamese-American photographer based in the Orange County area and a little bit of the Los Angeles Area. I had a passion for photography ever since senior year of high school in which I loved creating and designing beautiful imagery! My story began at Fountain Valley High School where I first learned about pin-hole cameras, black-and-white film photography, manual mode photography involving shutter speed and aperture, and then a little bit of digital photography. From then on, I continued my photo journey in college and as a free-lance photographer. Through my college years, I learned a hard truth.

Photography is not about picking up a camera and then just snapping pictures away.

Photography is about looking through your lens and composing your pictures with different angles and stories.

I believe mankind attracts us to mother nature and the beauty behind its wonderful world. That is why I love taking pictures of nature, landscapes, and lifestyle. I am always looking for new stories, a new chapter, and starting a new journey!
