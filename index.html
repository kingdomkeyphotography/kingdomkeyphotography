Our Mission

Kingdom Key Photography involves independent free lancing photography that surves the Orange County and Los Angeles Areas and sometimes other states or out of country. I strive to tell a story behind our pictures and capture the beauty behind colorful and magestic nature.

About Me

My name is Kevin Nguyen and I am currently a student at California State University, Long Beach majoring in Communication Studies with a minor in Web Design. Outside of school, I am a photographer at the Disneyland Resort and my hobbies include reviewing technology, free-lance photography, and exploration! I mainly like to photograph landscapes and lifestyle photography.
